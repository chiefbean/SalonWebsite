Hannah

Hannah Felice (she/her) has had a vibrant decade in the beauty industry. It began with graduating at the top of her class- having had several state and international styling competitions already under her belt. From there she continued to utilize her skills on the runway by organizing, curating, and styling live art fashion shows including Rogue Collections, RAW, and Minnesota Fashion Week. Finally, she has held her own as Minneapolis' resident hair nerd behind the chair with her ability to translate non hair speak and sometimes vague ideas into real life style. She enjoys building texture, taking on color projects, balayage, and has a slight obsession with micro foiling. She's an artist, a story teller, a secret keeper, and your favorite hair stylist.
