Audra

Making a career switch at 30 is never easy, but that's exactly what Audra did 6 years ago. Following her passion, she graduated the Aveda Institute Minneapolis at the top of her class. She has spent the last 6 years perfecting her craft. Whether it's a small hands on class, leading a segment for Fashion Week Minnesota, or helping curate a beautiful editorial photo shoot she takes every opportunity to learn new techniques and knowledge of the beauty industry. She's a hairstylist who specializes in everything blonde, color corrections, and creating shape and texture with every hair cut, but her ultimate goal with every person who sits in her chair is to help them feel like their insides match their outsides.
